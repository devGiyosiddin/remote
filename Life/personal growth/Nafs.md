## 📌 Nafs turlari va ularni jilovlash usullari

**Nafs** – odamning ichki dunyosidagi ruhiy ehtiroslar va istaklardir. Qur’onda nafs turli bosqichlari qayd etilgan. Masalan, Yusuf (12:53) surasida Alloh “_nafs al-ammāra bi’s-su_” (yomonlikka buyridigan nafs) haqida so‘zlaydi[quran.mto.org](https://quran.mto.org/stages-of-the-nafs/#:~:text=The%20Quranic%20expression%20an,the%20Sufi%20way%20of%20purification). Qiyomat (75:2) surasida esa **Nafs al-lawwāmah** (gunohdan o‘zini ayblaydigan nafs)ga qasam ichiladi[quran.mto.org](https://quran.mto.org/stages-of-the-nafs/#:~:text=The%20holy%20book%20contains%20also,man%E2%80%99s%20actions%20and%20controls%20him). To‘kinlik va poklik darajasidagi nafs – **Nafs al-mutma’innah** – “Ey tinchlanib qolganning nafsi!” deb ta’riflanadi (Fajr 89:27)
